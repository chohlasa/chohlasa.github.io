Mapping Undocumented Residents of New York City

Alex Chohlas-Wood, Frank Zha, Gang Zhao, Jane Stewart Adams

NYU CUSP

We have estimated the size and location of undocumented residents in New York City down to the census tract level. These estimates are available in sum and split by country of origin.

We have a custom interactive map -- along with descriptive methodology if you'd like to replicate our method -- available for anyone to use upon request.

Do you have good use for our data? Email us! We don't bite.
